Welcome to the

World of spices

Shudh Khao, Swasth Raho

The journey was started in

1957

with a dream to serve the market with pure and hygienic packed spices and food products. This foresight was efficiently guided by Late. Shri. Dhannalal Jain (Kala). He encouraged the team to work with an aim to serve the market with consistency, in quality and services. Hard work and patience paid during the process.

JK Spices was one of the first companies to introduce Commercial packed spices in small packaging. Its an honor to inform, JK Spices had initiated to install automatic Cleaning & Sortex Plant of Buhler.

Our Products

Our product portfolio includes a wide range of whole, ground, blended and processed spices and herbs with quality, credibility and consistency matching global standards.

We blend various spice mixes with customized combinations. Also included are other health food and wellness products such as Psyllium Husk, Soya Chunks, Tapioca Seeds and CTC Assam Tea on our portfolio.

Infrastructure

We use a combination of traditional and modern technology to retain the natural essence of spices. We are ceaselessly working towards building a class of products that are unparalleled in freshness, taste, quality and safety. We are empowered with:

Infrastructure | Technology | Work Force | Warehousing | Supply Chain

Therefore, the infrastructure of JK Spices is designed and constantly upgraded considering the importance of above mentioned factors.
